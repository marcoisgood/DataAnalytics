Analysis of Olymoic Games for sponsor¶

Analyzed by Marco Lin¶

Objective of the Proposed Research:¶

The purpose of this report is to find good athletes for the sponsors. The report will research four different aspects of data, such as region, Age, Weight, and Height. Before the investigation, I have assumed that the four aspects are related to the number of the medals.
The first one is the nation. I will explore which nation has numerous medals in history. The influence of country to an athlete may be better training, policy or the environment. Especially, the war in history may cause some countries to miss the Olympic games. So, I will also compare with the data after 2004.
Next, I will show the relationship between age and athletes. Age may be a contributing factor to the change of the athlete's physical status, thus affect his/her ability to win sports games.
Additionally, I will also consider the physical data of athletes. I believe that their height and weight play a key role in their performance of games.
In conclusion, I will integrate the data from those different aspects and find the character of good players that can help sponsor to choose good athletes.
